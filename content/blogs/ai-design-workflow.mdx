## AI is acceleration, not authorship

AI can compress synthesis, generate alternative layouts, pressure-test flows, and reveal edge cases. But it cannot decide what the product should feel like for a real user in a real context.

## Where it helps

- Turning messy stakeholder notes into early user stories.
- Exploring multiple layout directions quickly.
- Stress-testing edge cases before Figma polish.
- Drafting precise microcopy variants.

## Where I stay hands-on

Information hierarchy, visual rhythm, interaction priority, and taste still need a designer. AI can show options, but judgement decides what survives.
