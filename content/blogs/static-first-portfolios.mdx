## Keep the site boring under the hood

A portfolio rarely needs a backend. Most pages are written, reviewed, and published intentionally. That makes MDX and static generation a very good fit.

## Why MDX works

MDX keeps writing portable while still allowing custom components for case-study blocks, image treatments, and callouts.

The important part is restraint: content should stay mostly content. Components should exist when they clarify the story.

## Performance wins

Static generation keeps the page fast, predictable, and easy to deploy. The expensive thinking happens at build time, not on every visitor request.
