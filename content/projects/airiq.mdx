## Context

Air IQ is a B2B flight booking product for travel agents. As a Product Design Intern, I designed key booking flows across responsive desktop and mobile interfaces.

The work covered itinerary search, booking, confirmation, product requirement refinement, and improvements to the end-to-end booking workflow.

## The Problem

Travel agents compare fares quickly, often under pressure from customers. The product needed clear search and booking flows that helped agents move from itinerary details to confirmation without unnecessary friction.

> Speed is survival. Every extra decision point can slow a booking.

## Design Direction

The interface prioritised information hierarchy, responsive booking steps, and dense but readable flight information. I worked with product managers to refine requirements and connect the flow from search to booking to confirmation.

## Outcome

- Itinerary search, booking, and confirmation flows designed for desktop and mobile.
- Requirements refined with product managers.
- Booking workflow improved around travel-agent tasks.
- B2B product experience structured for speed and clarity.

## Learning

The strongest design decision was killing the modal filter early. Keeping filters visible beside results preserved the compare-filter-compare loop that agents rely on.
