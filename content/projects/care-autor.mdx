## Overview

Care Autor was a freelance product design project for a B2C healthcare platform focused on Alzheimer's and Autism care support. The challenge was translating sensitive care requirements into user-centered modules and interaction flows.

## Approach

I worked with stakeholders on UX research and product workflows, then structured the experience around role-based needs, low-friction onboarding, and accessible interaction patterns.

## System

The UI uses repeatable cards, gentle hierarchy, and direct copy. Caregiver tasks, patient status, and support routines are separated enough to reduce confusion but still feel like one product.

## Result

The project established a clear product foundation for a complex healthcare idea, with accessibility, emotional clarity, and user-centered decision-making treated as core requirements.
